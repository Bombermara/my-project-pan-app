Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the My Project Pan app collects, uses, and protects user data. This policy applies to all versions of the app available on both the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store.

1. Data Collected

The app does not collect any sensitive personal data. Only the following user-provided or local data may be stored:

  • Products manually entered by the user (name, brand, category);
  • Personalized routines and reminders;
  • Language, theme, and premium preference settings.

Any synchronized data (e.g., via Supabase or other cloud services) is anonymized and used solely to ensure proper app functionality.

2. Analytics and Advertising

The app may use analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics) or ad networks (e.g., Google AdMob) to measure usage and display contextual ads. No personally identifiable information is shared with third parties.

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

All transactions are handled securely through the official systems of the Apple App Store and Google Play Billing. My Project Pan never stores credit card numbers or payment information.

4. Notifications

The app may send local notifications to remind users of product expirations or routines. Notifications can be disabled at any time in the device settings.
