Privacy Policy - My Project Pan

Last Updated: August 20, 2025
Version: 2.0 (Updated for Apple App Store Compliance)
Important Notice: This Privacy Policy has been updated to comply with Apple's requirements for apps with auto-renewable subscriptions. Please read all sections carefully.

1. Introduction

My Project Pan ("we", "our app", "the service") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data provided directly by you

  • Skincare products: Name, brand, expiration date, personal notes
  • Beauty routines: Steps, times, frequency
  • Wishlist: Desired products and related notes
  • Photos: Product images (optional)
  • App preferences: Notification settings, theme, language

2.2 Subscription and purchase data

  • Purchase information: Subscription type, purchase date, payment status
  • Transaction history: Managed directly by Apple through StoreKit
  • Purchase receipts: Verified through App Store to validate subscriptions

2.3 Automatically collected data

  • Usage data: Features used, usage time, access frequency
  • Device data: Device model, iOS version, unique identifiers
  • Crash data: Technical logs to improve app stability
  • Advertising data: Ad interactions (Basic users only)

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 App functionality

  • Save and sync your products, routines, and wishlist
  • Send notifications for product expiration and routines
  • Personalize user experience
  • Provide statistics and insights about your products

3.2 Subscription management

  • Verify your subscription status
  • Enable premium features
  • Manage cross-device synchronization
  • Provide customer support for billing issues

3.3 Service improvement

  • Analyze usage to improve features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

4. Data Sharing

Fundamental principle: We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4.1 Apple and App Store

  • Subscription management: Apple manages all payments and subscriptions
  • Purchase verification: We communicate with Apple servers to verify receipts
  • Analytics: We use App Store Connect for anonymous statistics

4.2 Advertising service providers (Basic users only)

  • Google AdMob: To show personalized advertisements
  • Data shared: Advertising identifiers, app usage data
  • Purpose: Improve ad relevance

4.3 Backend and storage services

  • Supabase: For secure data backup (encrypted)
  • iCloud: For automatic synchronization between Apple devices

5. Data Security

5.1 Protection measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Limited access: Only authorized personnel can access systems
  • Secure authentication: We use standard security protocols
  • Secure backups: Protected and encrypted backup copies

5.2 Payment protection

  • All payments are handled directly by Apple
  • We do not collect or store payment data
  • We only use Apple's secure StoreKit services

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

6.1 Fundamental rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in transferable format
  • Objection: Object to processing for specific purposes

6.2 How to exercise rights

  • Send a request via email to: myprojectpan93@gmail.com
  • Use the "Delete Account" function in app settings
  • We will respond within 30 days of the request

7. Cookie and Advertising Management

7.1 Personalized advertising (Basic users)

  • We use Google AdMob to show relevant ads
  • You can disable personalized advertising in iOS settings
  • Premium/Pro/Lifetime users do not see advertisements

7.2 Advertising control

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • In-app: Consent preferences are managed automatically

8. Subscription-Specific Information

8.1 Billing data

  • All payments are processed by Apple
  • We do not have access to your payment data
  • Apple only shares subscription status with us

8.2 Subscription management

  • Cancellation: Manage through iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Refunds: Request directly from Apple through reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Support: Contact us for technical issues, not billing

9. Data Retention

9.1 Retention duration

  • Active accounts: Data retained while you use the app
  • Deleted accounts: Data deleted within 30 days
  • Crash data: Retained for 90 days for debugging
  • Analytics data: Anonymized after 24 months

10. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed in countries other than your residence, including the United States (for Apple and Google services). All transfers occur with adequate protection measures compliant with GDPR.

11. Children

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

  • We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy
  • Substantial changes will be notified through the app
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes
  • The current version is always available on this page

13. Regulatory Compliance

13.1 Applicable laws

  • GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation (EU)
  • CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act (for California users)
  • Apple App Store: Apple's privacy guidelines
